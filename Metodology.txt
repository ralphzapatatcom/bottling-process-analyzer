📊 Methodology
The system applies a 2-sigma (2σ) tolerance threshold. Any bottle exceeding the target_volume + (2 * std_deviation) is flagged as a critical overfill. This data is then exported to sensor_adjustment_log.csv for technical intervention.

Developed for industrial process optimization and quality control automation.
